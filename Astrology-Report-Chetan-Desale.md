Sade Sati is the 7.5-year transit of Saturn over and around the natal Moon. It occurs when Saturn transits through the 12th, 1st, and 2nd house from the Moon sign. For your Pisces Moon, this means Saturn transiting through Aquarius, Pisces, and Aries.

- **Maturity acceleration:** Sade Sati ages the soul. You will emerge from this period significantly more mature, resilient, and self-aware than you entered it.

Your chart has several features that significantly reduce the harshness of Sade Sati:
